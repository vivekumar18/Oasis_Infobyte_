Prashant Kishor

The Master Strategist of Indian Politics

The Political Game-Changer

Prashant Kishor is an Indian political strategist, founder of the Indian Political Action Committee (I-PAC). He has worked with several prominent political leaders and parties across India, helping shape election-winning campaigns.

Known for his data-driven approach to politics, Kishor revolutionized election campaigning in India by introducing professional management techniques, detailed voter analytics, and strategic communication methods.

Despite being a non-political background (former public health expert with UN), he became one of the most sought-after strategists in Indian politics, working across party lines.

"In politics, perception is the only reality. What people believe to be true matters more than what is actually true."

— Prashant Kishor
